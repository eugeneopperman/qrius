Getting started with Qrius Codes is quick and easy. Whether you prefer email, Google, or GitHub, we've got you covered.

## Sign Up Options

You have three ways to create your Qrius Codes account:

### Email & Password

1. Visit [Qrius Codes](https://qriuscodes.com) and click **Start Free** in the header
2. Click the **Sign Up** tab (if you see Sign In)
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (at least 8 characters recommended)
5. Click **Create Account**

### Google OAuth

1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage
2. Select **Continue with Google**
3. Choose or sign in to your Google account
4. Approve the permission to access your email

### GitHub OAuth

1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage
2. Select **Continue with GitHub**
3. Sign in to GitHub if prompted
4. Authorize Qrius Codes to access your GitHub account

## Email Verification

After signing up with email, check your inbox for a verification email from Qrius Codes. Click the verification link to confirm your email address. This usually takes a few seconds, but check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a minute.

**Note:** OAuth signups (Google and GitHub) skip email verification since we trust those providers to verify your identity.

## What Happens After Sign Up?

Once your account is verified, you'll land on the **Onboarding Wizard**. This quick walkthrough introduces you to the core features:

- Choose your first QR code type (URL, text, phone, WiFi, etc.)
- Learn how to customize colors and add logos
- Preview and download your QR code

You can skip the wizard and head straight to your dashboard by clicking **Skip** or the Qrius logo.

## Your Free Plan Starts Now

When you sign up, you automatically get a **Free plan** with:
- 5 dynamic QR codes
- Unlimited scans
- PNG downloads only
- 7-day scan history

Want more? Check out our [Pricing](/pricing) page to upgrade to Starter, Pro, or Business plans.

## Troubleshooting Sign Up

**"Email already in use"?**
You may have signed up before. Try **Sign In** instead, or use "Forgot Password" to reset your password.

**Didn't get a verification email?**
Check your spam folder. You can also request a new verification email from the sign-up page.

**OAuth won't connect?**
Make sure you're logged into your Google or GitHub account in another tab, then try again.
