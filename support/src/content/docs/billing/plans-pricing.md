## Free Plan

Perfect for trying Qrius out. Create up to 5 dynamic QR codes, track scans for 7 days, and export as PNG. Everything works — no credit card needed, no expiration. Genuinely free, forever.

**Best for:** Experimenting, personal projects, or seeing if QR codes solve your problem.

## Starter ($12/month)

Entry point for small campaigns. You get 50 QR codes, 90 days of scan history, and SVG exports alongside PNG. Handy if you're running a few campaigns or testing things out beyond the free tier.

**Best for:** Small businesses, freelancers, or anyone running light QR campaigns.

## Pro ($29/month)

The sweet spot for growing teams. 500 codes, a full year of scan history, PDF exports, team collaboration (5 members), an API for automation, and unlimited brand templates. Custom domain support means you can use your own short link.

**Best for:** Marketing teams, SaaS companies, or anyone doing serious QR tracking and design work.

## Business ($79/month)

Enterprise-grade for large organizations. Unlimited QR codes, unlimited history, white-label branding (remove Qrius branding entirely), up to 25 team members, and 10K daily API requests. Your own infrastructure.

**Best for:** Agencies, large enterprises, or anyone needing complete customization.

## Annual Billing Saves 22%

Choose annual billing at checkout and save roughly 22% compared to month-to-month. Lock in a lower rate and simplify budgeting.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there a free trial for paid plans?

No trial needed. The free plan is genuinely useful — 5 QR codes, scan tracking, PNG exports. Try it risk-free and upgrade when you're ready.

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade instantly and we'll prorate charges. Downgrade and your features stay until the end of your billing period. No penalties.

### What counts as a "dynamic" QR code?

Any code where you can change the destination URL and scans get tracked. All Qrius codes are dynamic — that's the whole point.

### Can I use Qrius codes commercially?

Absolutely. Every plan, including free, allows commercial use. Build products, run marketing campaigns, resell to clients — go for it.
