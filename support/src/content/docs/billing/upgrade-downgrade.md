You can change your plan whenever you want. No long-term contracts, no penalties. Here's how it works.

## How to Upgrade Your Plan

1. Sign in to your Qrius account.
2. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
3. Click **Upgrade** on the plan you want.
4. Review the cost (we'll prorate any charges) and confirm your payment.
5. Done. Your new features are live immediately.

### Prorated Charges on Upgrade

If you upgrade mid-month, we charge only for the remaining days at your new plan's rate. For example:

- You're on the **Free** plan ($0/month).
- On day 15 of your month, you upgrade to **Pro** ($29/month).
- You're charged roughly $14.50 — half the monthly cost for the remaining days.

Your next billing date stays the same. After this month, you'll be charged the full Pro amount on day 1 of your cycle.

## How to Downgrade Your Plan

1. Sign in and go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
2. Click **Downgrade** on the plan you want to switch to.
3. Confirm the change.
4. Your downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

### What Happens to Your QR Codes When You Downgrade?

**Your codes keep working.** Period. Even if you downgrade from Pro (500 codes) to Free (5 codes), all your existing QR codes continue to scan and track data.

The limit only applies to *new* codes. So if you have 100 active codes and downgrade to Free:
- All 100 codes keep scanning.
- You just can't create any new ones until you delete some and get below the limit.
- Once you're under the limit again, you can create more.

### How Long Until Downgrade Takes Effect?

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. So if you downgrade on day 5 of a 30-day cycle, you keep your current plan features until day 30. Then you switch to the lower plan.

## Staying on Your Current Plan

If you're happy where you are, you don't need to do anything. Your subscription renews automatically on your billing date. You'll see a confirmation email a few days before each renewal.

## Switching Between Plans of the Same Price

Need to stay at $29/month but switch from Pro to a hypothetical "Pro Plus"? Just contact us — we'll handle the switch without re-billing you.

## Common Scenarios

### "I upgraded but want to go back to Free."

Downgrade anytime. Your paid features stay until the end of your billing period, then you'll be on Free with all the Free limits. Your existing codes keep working.

### "I upgraded for a campaign but don't need the higher tier anymore."

Downgrade at any time. No refund for the current period, but you'll stop paying the higher rate after your cycle ends.

### "I'm on Free and almost hit my 5-code limit. Should I upgrade?"

If you just need a few more codes, delete one of your test codes or pause it. If you're genuinely running 5+ active codes regularly, Starter ($12/month) for 50 codes is a solid move.

### "What if I forget and my subscription renews?"

You have 14 days to request a refund. See our [Refund Policy](./refunds.md).
